Introduction

Nestled in a tranquil setting just half a mile from the charming village of Altarnun, Woodcroft offers the perfect escape for those seeking peace and relaxation.

Tucked away in the secret village of Treween, this hidden gem invites you to slow down and immerse yourself in its calming atmosphere. Surrounded by breathtaking natural beauty, with sweeping moorland on one side and the dramatic North Cornwall coast on the other: Woodcroft is the ultimate hideaway.

Whether you're looking for gentle countryside walks straight from your doorstep or coastal adventures just a short drive away, this retreat has it all. Lazy days unfold effortlessly here, leaving you refreshed and renewed.

Location

Stargaze in the UK's first international dark sky landscape, Bodmin Moor, containing Cornwall's highest peak: Brown Willy. The property is situated near Altarnun and a local pub serving hearty meals and local ales.

Visit Du Maurier's atmospheric Jamaica Inn, uncover the Dozmary Pool, where Arthurian folklore tells of Excalibur's resting place, or explore the dramatic filming locations for Doc Martin and Poldark in nearby Port Isaac and beyond.

Beyond the Moors, North Cornwall's rugged and dramatic coastline stands proudly among the worlds finest. Surf the waves at Bude, traverse cliff-top walks, and explore the charming fishing villages of Boscastle and Tintagel.

Facilities

The property has 3 bedrooms, one with a king, one with a double, and one with a double bunk bed..

The main bathroom and the master en-suite both feature a double rain shower with unlacquered brass fittings.

The kitchen is well equipped and includes an induction hob, an electric double oven and microwave, toaster, coffeemaker, dishwasher, fridge, and a freezer. The utility room also houses a sink and a washer dryer.

The lounge features a smart TV, a Google Nest, Nintendo Switch, record player, speaker system and a woodburner.

Outside

The garden borders a beautiful grazing field, providing a serene backdrop for dining outside on the patio. The decking area contains Adirondack chairs and a bench for relaxation.

A hot tub is available for guests to enjoy a soak under the stars with an outdoor shower to rinse off afterwards.

The front drive has space for parking up to 3 cars, and provides electric car charging for a Type 2 charger.
